Welcome to CrossRoads Tutoring

Our Mission is to Challenge, Uplift, and Inspire Students.

CrossRoads Tutoring's Goal is to make a difference in every single one our student's lives. The way we want to do that is by showing each student the amount of opportunities they have when they put in the work. We tutor in all levels of Mathematics, Computer Science, along with SAT and ACT Preparation. Not only do we tutor in the basic subjects, we also teach ways for their education to be applied in the real world, such as college planning, job searching, and even optimized learning and test taking techniques. I have been tutoring since 2018, and I have been blessed to have such amazing students and I truly feel like I have made an impact in each of their lives. CrossRoads Tutoring is not School #2. What I mean when I say that, is that our tutoring is exciting, fun, and personal all while being effective and efficient. We understand that if the current formal school system isn't working for that student, then we find more personal ways to make our students excited for tutoring which leads to a better understanding of the material every single time. Every student is different, such that every tutoring experience should be personalized by our tutors so it is the perfect environment to cultivate a new positive view towards learning. I believe that students cannot learn if they do not understand the importance of the topics, and if they are not having an enjoyable time with it. I can ensure that every tutoring session is personal, enjoyable, and focused. We promise at Crossroads Tutoring to always go above and beyond to every student that we make the commitment to tutoring.
